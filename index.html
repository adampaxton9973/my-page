Some notes that I have written

Here are a few reports that I have written during my graduate studies. Now of course I see all kinds of ways they could have been written better. Nonetheless I'm putting them up online in case they may be of use to someone :) I also think it's nice to put these up as they tell a bit of a story about my development as a graduate student.

Scattering and blow-up for semi-linear wave equations (pdf)

I wrote these notes in summer 2016, under the supervision of Luc Nguyen, whilst trying to learn about wave equations. Its mostly a compilation of well-known results on scattering of waves which can be found treated more comprehensively in books.

Compactness results for flowing to a harmonic map: pdf

I wrote this document on harmonic maps in spring 2016, under the supervision of Melanie Rupflin. It gives a brief introduction to harmonic maps from surfaces, focussing particularly on the phenomenon of "bubbling". There are some pretty pictures to illustrate bubbling, and we also proved a new compactness result for maps from degenerating Riemann surfaces, which is written up in Section 3.

Exotic Spheres: pdf

This is my Masters thesis, which was written at UCL in 2014/15, under the supervision of Jonny Evans. The goal was to give an exposition of John Milnor's famous construction of 7 dimensional "exotic spheres".
